# Biology Procedure - Slide 1

## Steps 1-5

**1.** Prepare the microscope and adjust lighting

**2.** Clean the glass slides with alcohol

**3.** Place specimen on the slide

**4.** Add a drop of water or staining solution

**5.** Cover with coverslip carefully

**6.** Place slide on microscope stage
