VINSERIA

A Nation Forged in Resilience

ABOUT

The Nation

Vinseria is a resilient nation built on the belief that strength comes from enduring hardship. Its rugged mountains and fortified cities reflect a history of survival, while its capital blends ancient structures with modern ones — showing how the past is strengthened, not replaced.

At its core, Vinserians value perseverance over talent, seeing failure as growth and unity as their foundation — making the country quiet, steady, and unbreakable.

02

Culture

🍲

Food

Hearty communal stews, stone-baked bread, and bitter mountain herbs. Meals are shared — eating alone is considered unusual.

🎶

Music

Deep drums, string instruments, and unison chanting. Songs retell stories of hardship overcome, sung at every major gathering.

🕯

Traditions

The Trial of Stillness — once a year, citizens spend a full day in silence to reflect. The most respected day in the Vinserian calendar.

03

Laws of Vinseria

🥗

Sunday lunch with family is mandatory — at least one green dish must be on the table.

🎤

Wearing a backwards cap in public is banned, unless you can sing the national anthem at full volume.

📸

Every citizen must own at least one plant and post it on social media every Saturday.

🌈

Once a week, free public transport for all — but only if you're wearing colorful clothing.

04

Tourist Attractions

🏔

Mount Servinius

Servinius is said to have discovered Vinseria from this peak after a grueling climb from the north. At that same moment, the other founders were arriving from different directions — turning the mountain into a symbol of endurance and shared fate.

🌊

Alden Bay

Vincentius arrived by sea, his ship entering this calm bay just as the others were reaching land elsewhere. The bay became known as the place of "quiet arrival," marking his role in the founding.

🌉

Barity Bridge

This marks the point where Barily first crossed into Vinseria from the west, immediately claiming it with bold confidence — only to discover the others had arrived at the exact same time. A detail that still gets joked about today.

05

"We Do Not Break."

In Vinseria, every fall is a lesson, every scar a medal. The people of Vinseria do not seek an easy path — they build one, stone by stone, together.
